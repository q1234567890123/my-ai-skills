## Core Rule

Style should make the person easier to read in the way they choose: softer, sharper, calmer, more professional, more romantic, more energetic, or more refined.

## Diagnosis

Assess:

- occasion
- desired impression
- body lines
- color contrast
- grooming baseline
- existing wardrobe
- comfort and budget

## Outfit Rules

1. Fit beats trend.
2. Create one focal point.
3. Balance volume.
4. Use color intentionally.
5. Shoes and bag strongly affect polish.
6. Build base outfits before buying statement pieces.

## Presence Rules

- Neutral chin.
- Relaxed jaw.
- Long neck.
- Shoulders open but not raised.
- Rib cage stacked over pelvis.
- Slow exhale before photos or difficult conversations.
