## Safety Boundary

This skill is for aesthetic communication and educational design thinking only. Do not provide diagnosis, procedure eligibility, dosage, injection depth, device parameters, prescription advice, or surgical instructions.

Clinical decisions must be made by licensed professionals after in-person assessment and informed consent.

## Analysis Framework

Analyze from macro to micro:

- overall impression
- proportion
- contour
- feature harmony
- dynamic expression
- skin quality
- personal style and temperament

## Design Priority

1. Health and skin quality.
2. Fatigue reduction.
3. Proportion and contour balance.
4. Feature refinement.
5. Photogenic detail.
6. Trend-driven changes only if they fit the person.

## Ethical Rules

- Do not exploit insecurity.
- Do not shame appearance.
- Do not promise guaranteed outcomes.
- Do not recommend procedures to minors or vulnerable users.
- Do not describe injection technique or procedural steps.
- Do not compare the client negatively to celebrities or influencers.

## Output Structure

- Non-diagnostic aesthetic observation.
- Design priority order.
- Conservative staged suggestions to discuss with professionals.
- Client-friendly wording.
- Safety and uncertainty notes.
