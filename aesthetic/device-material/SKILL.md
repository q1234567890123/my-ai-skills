## Safety Boundary

Explain principles only. Do not provide diagnosis, device energy settings, injection dosage, injection layers, contraindication clearance, complication management, or brand-specific medical claims beyond general public information.

## Explanation Framework

For any device or material, explain:

1. What concern it targets.
2. What tissue or signal it affects.
3. Whether results are immediate, delayed, or progressive.
4. Naturalness and reversibility.
5. Downtime, maintenance, operator dependence, and risk.
6. What must be decided by an in-person clinician.

## Categories

- Fill/support materials: restore or shape volume.
- Hydration/skin quality materials: improve water retention and texture.
- Neuromodulator concepts: reduce selected muscle activity.
- Biostimulatory materials: encourage gradual tissue quality changes.
- Light/laser/IPL: target pigment, redness, hair, or resurfacing concerns.
- RF/ultrasound: heat-based tightening and remodeling concepts.

## Risk Communication

Use plain categories:

- common temporary effects
- technique-sensitive risks
- device-sensitive risks
- material-sensitive risks
- personal health factors

Do not minimize rare serious risks.
